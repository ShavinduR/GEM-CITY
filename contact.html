The Story of FACET

FACET was born from the riverbeds of Ratnapura — the ancient City of Gems. For over two millennia, this valley has yielded the world's most prized sapphires, rubies, and alexandrites, mined by hand using traditions passed down through generations of families.

We bypass the layers of middlemen that inflate prices and obscure provenance. Every stone in our collection is sourced directly from the mine to your hands, ensuring you receive the true origin price alongside full certification from internationally recognised laboratories.

"We don't just sell gems; we share the heritage of Ceylon. Every stone is handpicked from the mines of Ratnapura."

Our guiding principle is simple: total transparency. You will receive the unedited stone, high-resolution videos, and the original laboratory certificate before any commitment is made.
